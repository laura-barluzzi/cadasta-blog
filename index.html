About this blog

Hi, thanks for visiting. I'm a self-taught koalacoder who is passionate about international development. Thanks to the Outreachy program I became a software developer intern at a tech-for-good organization called Cadasta. Here I'll be posting about my experience at Cadasta thoughout the summer 2017. I want to shed light on the reality of working for a tech-for-good, entirely-remote and open-source project.
